My Background

My story begins in Sacramento, California, where I was born the youngest of three. I have a sister who is 8 years older than me, and a brother who is 4 years older. My dad is a piano teacher, and my mom is a caricature artist. My parents' occupations catered to a creative and busy household.

Growing up, my siblings and I spent a lot of time drawing, playing music, running around, and learning math with our dad. Among many odd tricks, I learned how to juggle, unicycle, solve the Rubik's cube, and throw a frisbee four different ways. As I grew up, I was taught to love learning, and I believe that that contributed to my love for computer science and engineering. Wanting to help shape the future involves constantly learning, and that's something I always want to continue doing!
