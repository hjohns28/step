Helping Our Planet

One thing I'm super passionate about is the environment and helping to mitigate climate change.

Below are just a few lifestyle changes I have made, and that anyone can make, to be more sustainable! (Hover over the images to learn more :))

Eat plant-based. Going vegan, or just reducing meat, dairy, and egg consumption, is one of the greatest ways an individual can reduce their carbon footprint!
Say no to fast fashion. The fashion industry pollutes the ocean with microplastics, uses copious amounts of water, and adds to carbon pollution. Try buying second-hand or purchasing from sustainable companies!
Don't use single-use plastic. A simple change like using a reusable water bottle or taking reusable bags to the grocery store can make a big difference!
